Join us for Service Learning!

Our Objectives

To foster environmental stewardship and community engagement through hands-on beach park clean-up activities.

  • Raise awareness about the importance of preserving coastal ecosystems and protecting marine life.
  • Empower participants with practical skills in waste management and environmental conservation.
  • Create a sense of ownership and responsibility towards maintaining clean and healthy beaches for future generations.

    • During the workshop, Participants Will:

    1. Engage in beach/park litter collection and removal.
    2. Learn about the impact of marine/inland debris on coastal/terrestial ecosystems.
    3. Participate in educational discussions and hands-on workshops on waste reduction and recycling.

    Make a Difference While Learning!

    At our Beach Park Clean-ups, participants have the opportunity to make a tangible difference in their community while gaining valuable insights into environmental conservation. By actively participating in service learning projects, individuals not only develop a deeper understanding of environmental issues but also cultivate a sense of responsibility towards protecting our natural resources.

    Reinforced Learning Objectives: Circular Waste Management Classes

    After the clean-up, participants will have the opportunity to further reinforce their learning objectives through circular waste management classes. These classes will cover various aspects of recycling, including:

  • Plastic recycling through injection molding and extruders.
  • Paper recycling techniques.
  • Glass recycling processes.
  • Textile recycling methods.

    • By participating in these classes, participants will gain hands-on experience and practical knowledge in sustainable waste management practices, further enhancing their environmental literacy and commitment to conservation efforts.

    Ready to roll up your sleeves and make a positive impact? Join us for our next Beach Park Clean-up event! Whether you're a student, educator, or community member, there's a role for everyone in preserving our coastal environments. Together, let's work towards cleaner beaches and a healthier planet.

    Additional points to note

    At Terra Academy, we firmly believe in the power of experiential learning. To enrich our students' educational journey, we'll be venturing beyond the confines of traditional classrooms to embrace outdoor environments. However, with the inherent outdoor activities, there comes the potential risk of injuries and heat-related illnesses. Therefore, diligent supervision (or alternatively, attentive oversight) from our trained chaperones/teachers will be essential. This serves as an additional layer of protection for our participants, ensuring a safe and enriching experience for all involved.
