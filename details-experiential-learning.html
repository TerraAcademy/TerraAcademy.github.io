Embark on an Environmental Experiential Learning Journey!

Our Objectives

To immerse participants in hands-on environmental experiences that foster a deeper connection with nature.

  • Promote experiential learning opportunities that engage all senses and inspire curiosity.
  • Cultivate environmental awareness and appreciation through direct interactions with the natural world.
  • Encourage lifelong learning and stewardship by reflecting on every tactile moment, taking home a valuable lesson and story.

    • During the workshop, Participants Will:

    1. Engage in interactive discussions/lectures/activities that highlight ecological principles and biodiversity.
    2. Explore natural habitats through guided nature walks.
    3. Learn about local flora and fauna, ecosystems, and conservation practices.

    Discover Our Planet Through Experiences!

    At our Environmental Experiential Learning sessions, participants embark on a journey of discovery where every moment becomes a lesson and every experience a story. Through hands-on activities such as nature walks, individuals have the opportunity to connect with nature on a deeper level, gaining insights into environmental science, conservation, and sustainability.

    Ready to explore our planet and embark on an environmental learning adventure? Join us for our next Environmental Experiential Learning session! Whether you're a student, educator, or nature enthusiast, there's something for everyone to discover and learn from in the great outdoors.

    Contact us at hello@terraacademysg.com for more information.


    Additional points to note

    At Terra Academy, we firmly believe in the power of experiential learning. To enrich our students' educational journey, we'll be venturing beyond the confines of traditional classrooms to embrace outdoor environments. However, with the inherent outdoor activities, there comes the potential risk of injuries and heat-related illnesses. Therefore, diligent supervision (or alternatively, attentive oversight) from our trained chaperones/teachers will be essential. This serves as an additional layer of protection for our participants, ensuring a safe and enriching experience for all involved.
