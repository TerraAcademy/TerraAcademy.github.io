Curious About Marine Conservation and Intertidal Ecology?

Are you ever curious about what a Marine Conservationist or Intertidal Ecologist does? Or perhaps you're interested in learning how you can play a part in protecting our oceans? This is your chance to find out!

Topics Offered/Covered:

  • Marine Conservation: Learn about the work of Marine Conservationists and their efforts to protect marine ecosystems and wildlife.
  • Intertidal Ecology: Explore the fascinating world of intertidal zones and discover the diversity of life found in these unique habitats.
  • Marine Wildlife and Waste Management: Gain insights into the challenges facing marine wildlife due to pollution and learn about waste management solutions to mitigate environmental impacts.
  • Playing Your Role: Discover practical ways that you can contribute to marine conservation and environmental stewardship in your daily life.

    • Format of workshops:

    Guest lectures may not always be offered, but talks on playing your role in marine conservation and marine wildlife with waste management are always available. Drop us an email to customise a talk suited to your needs!

    Reinforced Learning Objectives: Circular Waste Management Classes

    After the Educational Talk, participants can have the opportunity to further reinforce their learning objectives through circular waste management classes. These classes will cover various aspects of recycling, including:

  • Plastic recycling through injection molding and extruders.
  • Paper recycling techniques.
  • Glass recycling processes.
  • Textile recycling methods.

    • By participating in these classes, participants will not only take home theoretical knowledge, but also gain hands-on experience and practical knowledge in sustainable waste management practices, further enhancing their environmental literacy and commitment to conservation efforts.

    Ready to expand your knowledge and make a difference for our oceans? Join us for our next Educational Talk! Whether you're a student, educator, or environmental enthusiast, there's something for everyone to learn and contribute to the protection of our marine ecosystems.

    Contact us at hello@terraacademysg.com for more information.


    Additional points to note

    At Terra Academy, we firmly believe in the power of experiential learning. To enrich our students' educational journey, we bring machines to facilitate hands-on activities and experiences. However, with the hands-on activities and the use of machinery, there comes the potential risk of injuries. Therefore, diligent supervision (or alternatively, attentive oversight) from our trained chaperones/teachers will be essential. This serves as an additional layer of protection for our participants, ensuring a safe and enriching experience for all involved.
